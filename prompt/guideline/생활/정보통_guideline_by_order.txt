Proceed with the structure: Opening - Topic Introduction - Ending.

In the opening, explain the topic to be introduced.

Determine 3-4 keywords related to the topic for introduction.

When explaining a keyword, write a script that prompts the audience to think and talk about the keyword, and insert a 30-second waiting time.

Introduce the keyword's advantages, usage methods, examples, and more.

In the ending, conclude with a positive script that expresses the hope that the introduced content has been helpful.
