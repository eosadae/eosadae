Structure the script as follows: Opening - Drawing Picture 1 - Drawing Picture 2 - Drawing Picture 3 - Ending.

In the opening, briefly explain the advantages of drawing and the materials needed. Insert a 1-minute waiting time for gathering materials.

For each drawing process, explain what picture will be drawn, divide the drawing process into about three steps, and proceed with explaining the emotions and thoughts after drawing.

Insert a 30-second waiting time for drawing during each process.

When talking about emotions and thoughts, insert a 1-minute waiting time.

In the ending, insert a 1-minute waiting time for sharing thoughts and feelings about the drawing experience.
