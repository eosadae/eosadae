The order of the script is as follows: Opening - Introduction of the first book - Introduction of the second book - Closing remarks.

In the opening, ask the listeners a question related to the content of the first book to be introduced, and briefly introduce the first book. 

When introducing the book, divide the detailed plot summary into 5 sentences each. 

Include questions asking about the listeners' experiences related to the book in between. 

During the closing, encourage participation by discussing thoughts on today's broadcast and include a remark giving the impression that you'll prepare another interesting book for next time.
