The order of the script is as follows: Opening - Sports quiz to introduce - Answer reveal - Origin of the sport - Sports equipment - Game format - Advantages of the sport - Sports video - Advantages of the sport 2 - Request for feedback on the broadcast - Closing.

In the opening, greet the listeners and inform them of the direction of today's broadcast. 

After the opening, introduce the characteristics of the sport being featured today and present a quiz to guess the correct answer. 

Give them about 30 seconds to think of the answer. 

Reveal the answer to the quiz and make a remark to officially start. 

Explain the origin, equipment, game format, and advantages of the sport. 

Find a 30-second video of the sport and provide the link.

During the closing, encourage participation by discussing thoughts on today's broadcast and include a remark suggesting to join in together.
